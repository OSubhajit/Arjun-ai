1. Information We Collect Minimal

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve the GitaPath service:

  • Account data: Your full name and email address when you register.
  • Authentication data: A bcrypt-hashed password (we never store your plain-text password).
  • Chat history: The messages you exchange with Arjun, stored to provide conversation continuity across sessions. A maximum of 50 recent exchanges are retained per user.
  • One-time passwords (OTPs): Temporary codes generated during registration or password reset. These are auto-deleted from our database within 10 minutes.
  • Technical logs: Server-side IP addresses recorded temporarily for rate-limiting and abuse prevention.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, biometric data, precise location data, or any sensitive personal information beyond the above.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing your GitaPath account.
  • Authenticating you securely when you log in.
  • Delivering personalised Bhagavad Gita guidance through Arjun by passing recent conversation context to the AI model.
  • Sending OTP verification emails during registration and password resets.
  • Preventing abuse, spam, and brute-force attacks through rate-limiting (IP-based, temporary).
  • Diagnosing and fixing technical errors through server-side logs.
We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing, profiling, or commercial purposes — ever.

3. Data Storage & Security Encrypted

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords: Hashed with bcrypt (adaptive cost factor) before storage. The original password is never logged or stored.
  • Database: Hosted on MongoDB Atlas with IP-allowlisting, TLS encryption in transit, and at-rest encryption.
  • Sessions: Managed via HTTP-only, SameSite=Lax, Secure (in production) cookies using server-side session signing. Sessions are invalidated immediately on logout.
  • Transport: All production traffic served over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). HTTP requests are redirected to HTTPS.
  • Security headers: All responses include X-Frame-Options: DENY, X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff, Content-Security-Policy, and Referrer-Policy.

No security measure is 100% foolproof. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users via email within 72 hours of becoming aware.

4. Cookies & Session Management

GitaPath uses a single first-party session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie:

  • Is HTTP-only (cannot be accessed by JavaScript).
  • Is flagged Secure in production (only transmitted over HTTPS).
  • Has SameSite=Lax to mitigate cross-site request forgery.
  • Expires after 7 days if "Remember me" is selected, or at the end of the browser session otherwise.
  • Is immediately invalidated when you click "Sign Out".

We do not use tracking cookies, fingerprinting, persistent advertising cookies, or any first-party analytics cookies. Third-party advertising cookies may be set by Google AdSense — see Section 6.

5. Third-Party Services

GitaPath integrates with the following third-party services to operate:

  • OpenRouter / OpenAI: Your message (and up to 5 prior exchanges) is sent to the OpenRouter API to generate Arjun's response. OpenRouter forwards this to an LLM provider (e.g. OpenAI GPT-3.5-Turbo). Your message is subject to OpenRouter's Privacy Policy.
  • MongoDB Atlas (MongoDB Inc.): Our database provider. Data is stored in their cloud infrastructure subject to MongoDB's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN, which may log your IP address per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Gmail via Google Apps Script: OTP emails are dispatched through a Google Apps Script relay. Email addresses used for OTP are not retained by the relay after dispatch.

6. Advertising (Google AdSense)

GitaPath displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and device identifiers to show you personalised ads based on your browsing history across sites.

  • Google's advertising practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • EU/EEA users may also opt out via the Your Online Choices tool.
  • GitaPath does not pass any account or chat data to Google AdSense.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data (name, email, hashed password): Retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Chat history: A rolling window of your 50 most recent exchanges is retained. Older messages are automatically purged.
  • OTP records: Auto-deleted by database TTL index within 10 minutes of creation.
  • Rate-limit records: Auto-deleted by database TTL index within 10–15 minutes.
  • Account deletion: To request deletion of your account and all associated data, email subhajitsarkar0708@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account". We will process the request within 30 days.
