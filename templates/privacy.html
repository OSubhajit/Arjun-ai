Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Data We Collect

We collect your name, email address, and hashed password when you register. We also store your chat history with Arjun to provide conversation continuity.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the GitaPath service — authentication, chat history, and personalised Gita guidance. We do not sell your data to any third party.

Cookies & Sessions

We use secure HTTP-only session cookies to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies are used for advertising purposes.

Advertising

This site may use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to personalise ads based on your browsing activity. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

Data Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text. All data is stored in MongoDB Atlas with access controls enforced.

Contact

For any privacy concerns, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository.
