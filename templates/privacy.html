1. Information We Collect Minimal

  • Account data: Your full name and email address when you register.
  • Authentication data: A bcrypt-hashed password (we never store your plain-text password).
  • Chat history: Messages you exchange with Arjun. A maximum of 50 recent exchanges are retained per user.
  • OTPs: Temporary codes auto-deleted within 10 minutes via database TTL index.
  • Technical logs: IP addresses recorded temporarily for rate-limiting and abuse prevention.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Creating and managing your account.
  • Authenticating you securely on login.
  • Delivering personalised AI guidance by passing recent conversation context to the AI model.
  • Sending OTP verification emails during registration and password resets.
  • Preventing abuse through rate-limiting.
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for any commercial purpose.

3. Data Storage & Security Encrypted

  • Passwords: Hashed with bcrypt before storage — the original password is never logged.
  • Database: MongoDB Atlas with TLS encryption in transit and at-rest encryption.
  • Sessions: HTTP-only, SameSite=Lax, Secure cookies; invalidated on logout.
  • Transport: All production traffic served over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Security headers: X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, CSP, and Referrer-Policy on every response.

4. Cookies & Session Management

GitaPath uses a single first-party session cookie to keep you logged in. It is HTTP-only, Secure (in production), SameSite=Lax, and expires after 7 days ("Remember me") or at session end.

We do not use tracking cookies or first-party analytics cookies. Third-party advertising cookies may be set by Google AdSense — see Section 6.

5. Third-Party Services

  • OpenRouter / OpenAI: Your message and up to 5 prior exchanges are sent to generate Arjun's response. Subject to OpenRouter's Privacy Policy.
  • MongoDB Atlas: Database provider. Subject to MongoDB's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Fonts loaded from Google CDN, which may log your IP. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Gmail via Google Apps Script: OTP emails dispatched through a relay. Addresses are not retained after dispatch.

6. Advertising (Google AdSense)

GitaPath may display advertisements via Google AdSense. You can opt out of personalised ads at Google Ad Settings. GitaPath does not pass account or chat data to Google AdSense.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active.
  • Chat history: Rolling window of 50 most recent exchanges; older messages are auto-purged.
  • OTPs & rate-limit records: Auto-deleted by MongoDB TTL indexes within 10–15 minutes.
  • Account deletion: Email subhajitsarkar0708@gmail.com with subject "Delete My Account". Processed within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict your data, depending on your jurisdiction. Contact subhajitsarkar0708@gmail.com — we will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

GitaPath is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal data, email us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect changes. Material changes will be notified via email to registered users.
